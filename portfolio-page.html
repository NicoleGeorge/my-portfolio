Hi, I’m Nicole, a former fashion designer turned creative coder from Adelaide, Australia. My jams are web design & copywriting, but I also cast spells with UI/UX design, illustration, brand strategy & content marketing.

My love for patternmaking & garment construction saw me leverage my creative and analytical side into a multi-passionate academic career, by undertaking a Master of Laws & Cyber Security.

When I’m not coding, you can often find me reading comic books or political thrillers, listening to Marina & The Diamonds while scrolling Instagram, drinking coffee out of pop culture mugs or watching the X-Files.
