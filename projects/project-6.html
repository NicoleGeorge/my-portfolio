As part of a group project for Adeaide University Coding Bootcamp, our task was to create a website using APIs. The group and I decided that 'our Client', Oz Comic Con, wanted to generate interest around Kit Harington as their special guest at the 2021 convention.

They wanted an app that will appeal to their newer fans as a way to research their favourite character and register for the competition. The app app was built with a CSS framework other than Botstrap Framework, 3 APIs with HTML/CSS dynamically powered by JavaScript.
